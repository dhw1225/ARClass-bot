## Guessing-game data

`guess_community_aliases.json` contains filtered community nicknames derived
from the [Arcaea Chinese Wiki page](https://wiki.arcaea.cn/index.php/User:WYXkk/曲目常见别称)
identified in the file's `_meta` object. The snapshot records its exact source
revision and the source site's CC BY-NC-SA terms.

`guess_aliases.json` contains separately reviewed aliases from multiple public
community references, including the public Yurisaki/Arcaea-AUA song database
and [Arcaea-Infinity/ArcaeaSongDatabase](https://github.com/Arcaea-Infinity/ArcaeaSongDatabase).
The Yurisaki source is available as
[Yuri-YuzuChaN/Arcaea-AUA](https://github.com/Yuri-YuzuChaN/Arcaea-AUA).
